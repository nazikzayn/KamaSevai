Connecting Sri Lakans with the best leftovers

Our mission is to supply all valuable leftovers in restaurants, reception halls and events to those who crave for a good meal. By doing so, we aim to achieve our vision of reducing food waste in Sri Lanka, which has been a drastic issue that has gone unadressed for far too long. Join us and Enjoy the delicious benefits !

What do we do for you

As a food provider

We know all food is meant to be consumed. Its all too good to go. We aim to give you a platform where you can send your leftovers to those in your area without having to waste it. This can all be for a good cause or to help anyone satisfy a meal at any time of the day.

As a consumer

We know you crave for a good meal at anytime. So we want to connect you with those who are willing to give you a valuable meal called "Untouched Leftovers". Remember, its all for a good cause !
